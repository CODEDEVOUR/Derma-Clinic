About Us

Derma Clinic, with the help of expert dentists and a strong team, was founded on 16th July 2001 at New Baneshwor. It is now one of the highly reputed dental hospitals in Kathmandu, known to provide instant yet qualitative and affordable dental treatment to its patients. The hospital is ideally located at New Baneshwor, opposite to Rastriya Banijya Bank. It is a government-registered hospital and accredited to national standards.
